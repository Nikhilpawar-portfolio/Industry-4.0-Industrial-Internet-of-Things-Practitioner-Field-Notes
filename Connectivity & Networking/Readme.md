# 🌐 Sensing, Connectivity & Networking

Foundational concepts behind how industrial systems sense, communicate
and exchange information.

This domain explores the technologies enabling machines, devices,
processes and industrial assets to become observable, connected and
digitally accessible.

---

## Why This Matters

Connectivity is the foundation of Industry 4.0.

Without reliable sensing, communication and networking, higher-level
capabilities such as analytics, traceability, AI, smart manufacturing
and autonomous decision-making cannot exist.
